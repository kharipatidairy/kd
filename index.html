ABOUT US

Kharipati Dairy Pvt. Ltd. was founded in 1995, beginning as a small cottage dairy. The company initially collected milk from local farmers and marketed it to consumers. Over the years, Kharipati Dairy has steadily grown and expanded its customer base. Today, we take pride in producing high-quality dairy products and have earned a strong reputation as the third-largest producer and distributor of pasteurized milk in the region.


At Kharipati Dairy, we are dedicated to supporting rural milk producers while ensuring urban consumers in Kathmandu, Bhaktapur, and Lalitpur have access to fresh, quality milk and milk products. We prioritize the interests of our rural suppliers by offering competitive prices and remain committed to delivering the highest quality products at the most reasonable cost.
